Cath's feel-Good movies

My top 3 movies

Captain America

Not only is Chris Evans insanely sexy in this, the construction and especially character development is amazingly done for a superhero movie. Each part either develops the characters and their relationships or adds to the stakes of the general story.

Tangled

My favorite Disney movie by far. The main character is siply adorable and the love interest is a lot less one-dimensional than in any other Disney film. Plus there's some self-awareness in this movie that makes it even funnier. Some of the songs are a little weak, but there aren't too many and the rest of the movie is actually good, wether you're an adult or a child.

Stardust

This movie has adventure, romance, humor, fantasy, really bad villains and even a unicorn. What else could you ask for?
