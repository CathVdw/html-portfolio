Cath's feel-good movies

The three movies I watch when I'm feeling really down

Captain America

A small, sickly guy wants to go to war and becomes a buff superhero in the process.

Stardust

After vowing to retrieve a fallen star, a young man crosses a hole in a wall and ends up in a magical kingdom where witches can conjure whole taverns out of nowhere, pirates steal lightning and the fallen star turns out to be a young woman with a temper.

Tangled

After being kidnapped, locked up and gaslit her entire life by a beauty-obsessed witch, a blond princess runs away, fall in love and becomes a brunette, all in one day.
